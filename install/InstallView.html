StayFocused

Thank you for installing StayFocused!

StayFocused is a free and open source extension that helps you stay focused on your work by blocking distracting websites.

Features

  • Block distracting websites
  • Block websites for a certain amount of time
  • Initiate Emergency Mode to block all websites

Permissions

  • Storage - to store your settings
  • Tabs (website data) - to block websites
⚠️ For StayFocused to block websites, it needs access to your website data (tabs). Click to provide your permission.
