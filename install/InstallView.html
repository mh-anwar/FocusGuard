StayFocused

Thank you for installing StayFocused!

StayFocused is a chrome extension that helps you stay focused on your work by blocking distracting websites.

StayFocused is a free extension that is open source and can be found on GitHub.

StayFocused is a work in progress and will be updated with new features and bug fixes.

Features

  • Block distracting websites
  • Block websites for a certain amount of time

Permissions

  • Storage - to store your data
  • Tabs - to block websites
For StayFocused to work it need access to your tabs. Click to provide your permission.
